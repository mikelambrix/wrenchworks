Our Story

Our passion and purpose is to keep your JDM vehicle
firing on all cylinders

Our founder has seen the popularity and reliability of Japanese vehicles in the Seattle area. As an experienced mechanic, Wrench Works was born to give consumers the dedicated service outside of the dealership.

With a focus on providing high-quality maintenance, repairs, and customization, the business is dedicated to meeting the specific needs of Japanese car owners. The shop combines expert knowledge of Japanese engineering with excellent customer service, offering both general and specialized auto services.
