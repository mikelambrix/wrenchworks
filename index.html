Services

Wrench Works utilizes OEM parts and fluids as recommended by each owners manual.

  • Oil Fluid &
    Filter Change

    Keep your engine clean and free of harmful build up with fresh oil as recommended by the manufacture.

  • Coolant System
    Flush

    Remove contamination that can cause overheating by flushing your coolant according to the manufacturer's maintenance schedule.

  • Spark Plug Replacement

    Replace your vehicle's spark plugs to enhance engine performance and ensure optimal combustion for the road ahead.

  • Check Engine
    Light

    Diagnose and repair your vehicle with our advanced diagnostic tools to help get you and your vehicle driving again.

  • Brake System Mainenance

    Inspect and replace worn brake pads, shoes, rotors and disks. Add a brake fluid flush to ensure smooth operation.

  • Battery Inspection
    & Replacement

    Check your battery's health, free of charge. Battery replacement will ensure you and your vehicle maintains factory reliability.
